The aim of our project is to prove that LightGBM (light Gradient Boosting Machine) is a better alternative than the famous XGBoost(eXtreme Gradient Boosting) in terms of speed. And if the hyperparameters are tuned properly, then the accuracy between the two models is almost negligible.

Note: The Training data was obtained from Github. The modules and results used here are subjected to change, for futher improvements.
